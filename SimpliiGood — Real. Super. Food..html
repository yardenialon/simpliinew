The science, simply

Frozen keeps it alive.

Most spirulina is dried and milled into a dusty powder. We freeze ours fresh instead — so it stays alive, potent, and (best of all) flavor-free.

Dried powder

Heat. Dust. Aftertaste.

Drying with heat strips delicate nutrients and leaves that unmistakable pond-y flavor most people learn to dread.

SimpliiGood frozen cube

Fresh. Potent. Flavor-free.

Freezing locks in the live nutrition and the bright phycocyanin antioxidant — with no taste to fight. Just drop, blend, go.
