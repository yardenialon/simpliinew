The breakthrough

We turned an algae into a canvas.

Spirulina is a complete protein with a natural structure we can shape. By working with its chlorophyll and fibres, we craft a clean, whole-food texture that slices, folds and melts like premium smoked salmon, without a single fish.

No farmed salmon. No microplastics. No heavy metals. Just real, traceable nutrition that chefs can plate and consumers can trust.
