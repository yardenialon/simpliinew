Why we exist

Most greens are dead on arrival.

The spirulina most people know is a dried, heat-processed powder that lost its life on the way to the shelf, harsh, swampy, hard to love. We refused to accept that.

So we kept ours fresh and flash-frozen, alive and whole, with no additives and no fishy taste. Real. Super. Food. The kind your body actually recognises.
